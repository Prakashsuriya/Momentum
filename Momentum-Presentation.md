## Slide 2: The Problem

# The Curiosity-to-Action Gap

- Learners consume inspiring content daily but struggle to convert curiosity into action
- Feel motivated after YouTube tutorials but don't know the first step to take
- Stuck in "tutorial hell" — constantly consuming but never creating
- **Target users:** Self-learners consuming YouTube, blogs, podcasts, online courses
- The gap is largest when motivation is high but clarity is low

**Why they fail:** Lack of structure and clear starting points

---

## Slide 4: The Solution

# Momentum

## Converts curiosity into structured 7-day action plans

**How it works:**
1. Enter your curiosity topic
2. Select time available (15 min → 2 hours)
3. Choose your goal
4. AI generates personalized execution sprint
5. Click "Start Day 1" to commit

**Result:** Transform passive curiosity into active learning
